Personas Plus Retirement Notification

Personas Plus is retiring on November 30th, 2018. After many years of helping users create and access themes, and following the modernization of theming technology for Firefox, it’s time to say goodbye to Personas Plus and to say hello to Firefox Color, Mozilla’s new tool for creating custom themes.

Firefox Color is a new extension that makes it easy for you to change the look of Firefox. You can choose different color combinations and textures for your own Firefox theme and share your creation with others.

To start using Firefox Color, install the extension from color.firefox.com. If you would like to create and publish a new theme, please go to the Developer Hub on addons.mozilla.org. If you enjoyed using the Favorites feature in Personas Plus to save your favorite themes, please create a new collection on addons.mozilla.org.
